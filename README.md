# Luna Labs
### EdgeAI Innovations For Humankind

Welcome to the official repository for **Luna Labs**, a Bangalore-based startup dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology. We engineer **Edge-AI integrated hardware** designed to empower and assist humankind.

---

## 🌍 Our Mission

At Luna Labs, we believe that intelligence should live where the action happens. By integrating advanced Artificial Intelligence directly into hardware at the edge, we are creating faster, more secure, and highly efficient solutions that adapt to the needs of the real world.

## 📍 Where We Are

Based in the heart of India's tech ecosystem—**Bangalore**—we draw inspiration from the city's vibrant engineering culture to build solutions for a global impact.

## 🛠 What We Do

We bridge the gap between complex AI models and physical hardware constraints. Our core focus areas include:

* **Edge-AI Optimization:** Running high-performance neural networks on power-constrained devices.
* **Integrated Hardware Design:** Developing custom hardware architectures that put AI at the center of the user experience.
* **Human-Centric Engineering:** Building technology that serves, assists, and protects.
